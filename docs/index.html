India · Built & Lit

A district-level panel of annual building volume (Google Open Buildings 2.5D Temporal) and monthly night-time lights (VIIRS DNB) for ~640 SHRUG / PC11 districts. Building data is computed server-side on Google Earth Engine; VIIRS is cleaned locally with NighttimeLights.jl.
